♦ Process ♦

1 -­ After we negotiate a price, I’ll send you the invoice and will start working after confirming the payment.

2 -­ Once the payment is confirmed, I'll need a detail description about the subject, the more information the better!!!

Example:

The character is a barbarian, with long dark hair, and long dark beard. He has several scars in his torso, and one scar in his right eye. He also doesn't have the left thumb (lost in battle). The background is a tavern with wooden walls, and wooden shields as decorations hanging on the walls. You can see the window, it is night, and it is raining outside. There is a lot of smoke, with a wild boar being roasted on the grill.

Also, You can send image references, explaining with part of the image you want me to use in which way.

Example:

Here’s an image of a hat that I would like to see something similar in my character.
Here’s an image of a motorcycle engine, I would like to incorporate some elements of this design in my character.

Everything that you don't include with specific information, I will use my artistic creativity and fill in the blanks. If you are not sure about what you want to do, we can have a chat too and I can offer some suggestions.

3 -­  The request will be done step by step and on each step, I’ll send you an update and ask for feedback. After it gets approved, I won't be coming back to change big things in later stages. Updates are made at least once a week.

4 -­  I'll only send the final file after the commission is fully paid.
